How to use Mapty
  • Click anywhere on the rendered map (as shown on the right side of the screen) to mark that place on the map as your potential workout place.
  • Once you have marked your potential workout place, you can see a marker placed on your chosen position.
  • Now, you can fill the form (as shown on the left side of the screen). Once done, click on the button to submit the form.
  • Once, submitted you can see your rendered workouts on the map and on the workouts display section (left part of the screen).
  • Using, the above instructions, now you can map your workouts and keep yourself updated with your workouts routine.
